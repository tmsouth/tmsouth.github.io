Robert Mahari

Boston, Massachusetts

Building the future of computational law

Robert Mahari is a PhD student at the MIT Media Lab advised by Sandy Pentland and holds a JD from Harvard Law School. He studies how technology can and should affect the practice of law with a focus on increasing access to justice and judicial efficacy.
